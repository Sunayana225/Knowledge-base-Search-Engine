Physics Formulas and Concepts

Motion:
- Velocity: v = d/t (distance over time)
- Acceleration: a = (v₂ - v₁)/t
- Force: F = ma (mass times acceleration)
- Momentum: p = mv (mass times velocity)

Energy:
- Kinetic energy: KE = ½mv²
- Potential energy: PE = mgh
- Work: W = Fd (force times distance)
- Power: P = W/t (work over time)

Electricity:
- Ohm's law: V = IR (voltage equals current times resistance)
- Power: P = VI (voltage times current)
- Energy: E = Pt (power times time)

Waves:
- Wave speed: v = fλ (frequency times wavelength)
- Period: T = 1/f (inverse of frequency)
