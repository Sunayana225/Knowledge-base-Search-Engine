How to Use the Knowledge-base Search Engine

A comprehensive guide to uploading documents and searching with AI-powered answers

🚀 Getting Started

The Knowledge-base Search Engine is a powerful tool that allows you to upload documents and search through them using AI-powered natural language queries. Instead of manually browsing through documents, simply ask questions in plain English and get intelligent answers backed by your documents.

✨ Key Benefit: Get instant, accurate answers from your documents without having to read through everything manually.

1Upload Your Documents

Begin by uploading the documents you want to search through.

  • Navigate to the Upload Section: Scroll down to the "Drag and drop files here" area on the home page.
  • Add Files: Either drag and drop files directly into the box, or click to browse and select files.
  • Supported Formats: PDF and TXT files are supported (max 50MB per file).
  • Wait for Processing: The system will automatically process your documents, extract text, and prepare them for searching. A progress bar shows the upload status.
💡 Tip: You can upload multiple documents at once. The system will process each one individually.

2View Your Documents

Once uploaded, your documents will appear in the Documents List section below the upload area.

  • Document Status: Each document shows its current status (Completed, Processing, or Failed).
  • Document Information: View the file name, upload date, and processing status.
  • Delete Documents: Remove documents from the knowledge base using the delete button if needed.

3Search with AI-Powered Answers

Use the search box at the top of the page to ask questions about your documents.

  • Enter Your Question: Type a natural language question in the search input (e.g., "What are the main points about machine learning?").
  • Click Search: Press the "Search" button or hit Enter to submit your query.
  • Receive AI Answer: The system searches through all uploaded documents and uses AI to generate a comprehensive answer.
  • See Sources: Below the answer, you'll find the source documents and relevant excerpts that support the answer.
💡 Tip: Ask specific, clear questions for better results. For example, instead of "Tell me about this", ask "What are the key benefits mentioned in the document?"

⭐ Best Practices

📄 Document Quality

Upload clear, well-formatted documents. Text should be legible and properly extracted from PDFs.

❓ Ask Clear Questions

Specific, well-phrased questions yield better results than vague queries.

🔍 Review Sources

Always check the source documents and excerpts to verify the AI's answer accuracy.

📚 Organize Documents

Keep your knowledge base organized by uploading related documents together for better context.

🎯 Key Features

  • AI-Powered Search: Uses advanced machine learning to understand your questions and find relevant information.
  • Source Attribution: Every answer shows where the information came from, so you can verify accuracy.
  • Multi-Document Support: Search across multiple documents simultaneously for comprehensive answers.
  • Semantic Understanding: The system understands meaning and context, not just keyword matching.
  • Fast Processing: Get instant answers even with large document collections.

🔧 Troubleshooting

  • Upload Failed: Check that your file is in a supported format (PDF or TXT) and under 50MB. Ensure the file isn't corrupted.
  • No Results: If a search returns no results, try rephrasing your question or ensure documents are fully processed.
  • Slow Response: Large document collections may take a moment to search. Be patient for the results.
  • Unclear Answers: For better results, ensure your documents contain relevant information about your query topic.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

  • How many documents can I upload? You can upload as many documents as you need. There's no limit on the number of documents.
  • Can I update or replace documents? Delete the old document and upload the new version.
  • Is my data private? All documents are stored securely. Read the Privacy policy for details.
  • What's the maximum document size? Individual files can be up to 50MB.
  • How accurate are the answers? The accuracy depends on your document content and question clarity. Always verify important information against the source documents.

📞 Need Help?

If you encounter any issues or have questions not covered here:

  • Check the troubleshooting section above.
  • Review the source documents referenced in search results for accuracy.
  • Try rephrasing your question for better results.
  • Contact support if you believe there's a technical issue.
